Solid STEM Foundation seeks to raise awareness on Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics by providing trainings and
exposure that inspires, engages, challenges and rewards young brilliant minds from underserved communities. We give early-stage
STEM exposure thereby igniting a spark that causes young children (especially girls) from lowincome communities to be steered in
the direction of innovation, while ensuring that they have the right mentorship and exposure to make them world class STEM professionals.
To provide STEM awareness and increase accessibility to practical and qualitySTEM programs, in low-income and underserved communities in Nigeria. We use Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) exposure, and increased technology literacy to inspire, and groom marginalized populations to thrive in fast-paced environments.
STEM professionals from minority backgrounds, unhindered by systemic and economic barriers, thriving and birthing world-class innovation that emerged from having a Solid foundation in STEM.

What We Do?

Curriculum

We design and implement quality practical learning programs for children, that do not have exposure to science and technology materials,through the inauguration of STEM clubs in schools.

Workshops

We deliver creative, free, hands-on and team-based STEM workshops/bootcamps on various STEM topics t ostudents (focus group: Age 9-15) in low income and underserved communities across Nigeria

Scholarships

We award scholarships to promising and underprivileged school students to ensure they make it into university and have
the appropriate mentorship.

Build

We build confidence and encourage young girls to develop an interest in STEM, empowering them and connecting them to mentors and opportunities in STEM.
